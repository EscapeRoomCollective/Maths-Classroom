You are trapped in a typical-looking Maths classroom. You see some desks arranged in a strange layout, a projector and projector screen, an overflowing waste bin, and a locked cabinet in the corner. There is also a large locked door to one side.

The projector is currently off.

The projector whirrs into life and projects some symbols towards the screen.

The screen displays nothing, since the projector is off.

Now that the projector is on, the screen displays some half-written symbols. The rest of the symbols must be somewhere...

Behind the screen you see some half-finished symbols written on the wall. The rest of the symbols must be somewhere...

There are lines written on the wall, combining with the line from the projector, spelling out a code in four colours!

The door is locked and next to it, you spot an unusual keypad.

The door swings open and you stroll out into the sunlight, free at last!

Congratulations! You have completed the game!

Beneath some pieces of scrunched up paper, you spot 2 AA batteries.

The cabinet is locked with a 3 digit padlock.

The door swings open, revealing a rather bare cabinet, with a Projector Remote on the middle shelf.

The desks are arranged in an unusual pattern.
